2019's Joker is a film Staring Joaquin Pheonix as Arthur Fleck. The movie is about A mentally disturbed man who works full-time as a clown entertainer and has an ambition of becoming a comedian, but, through several hardships in the brutal, crime-filled city of Gotham, he eventually becomes the famous clown prince of crime, the Joker. This film is written and directed to be an origin story for the famous villian and has recived numerous awards and overwhelming praise for its great acting, cinematography, and its writing.
